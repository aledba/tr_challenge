Key Insights & Takeaways

1

Sample size is the strongest predictor of model performance

Postures with 500+ samples achieve F1 > 0.92; rare postures (<50 samples) struggle below 0.50

2

TF-IDF baseline is competitive - simple models should not be dismissed

Outperformed the transformer on all metrics except recall. Legal domain has strong keyword signals.

3

Document length is a real engineering challenge

Median 2,693 tokens. Only 4.1% fit BERT. Summarization adds complexity and information loss.

4

Multi-label nature adds significant complexity

49.8% multi-label docs. Labels encode orthogonal dimensions (stage, motion, proceeding).

Business Recommendation

Partial automation is feasible for high-frequency postures. A tiered deployment strategy (auto / assisted / human) can yield ~34% efficiency gains while maintaining quality.
